Microsite for IDBA
In this case, as part of the UX/UI Design Bootcamp at IronHack, the assignment was to design a microsite for any event that we chose. 

I was looking for something that inspired me, as for that I started looking for events that I had already visited in the past. In 2018 I had the opportunity to attend to the Indie Developer Burguer Awards as a representative of the indie studio Kraftum Games and I loved it. SiemensPlayground
Everything started when Siemens Mobility was building a new type of office, where workers could go to work with their kids.
That is when they found the necessity to build an app to entertain the kids and at the same time show what their parents do at work.The app would be installed on the office iPads and used by kids between 4 and 8 years old.

Alexandre Mauro and I team up and started together this 12 days assignment as our las project in the Ironhack UX/UI Bootcamp


Stock used: Sketch, Illustrator, Principle.
